Top three activities to do at Sankt Augustin

Attend a concert of our wind orchestra

The orchestra "Siegklang" is an great wind orchestra. All of his musicians are amateurs.

Enjoy the silence in the nature

The banks of the river Sieg are perfect for piknic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Study at one of the best schools of Germany

The Hochschule Bonn-Rhein-Sieg, University of Applied Sciences (H-BRS) is a dynamic and research-driven university with more than 9,000 students.
